PRINCIPAL
GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE, HASSAN

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you all to GECH Utsava 2026 — our annual celebration of talent, creativity and the indomitable spirit of youth. Events like Utsava are a reminder that education is not just about textbooks; it is about discovering oneself, building bonds and contributing to a larger community. I wish all participants the very best and hope this festival inspires each one of you to dream bigger.
